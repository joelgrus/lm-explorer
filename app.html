GPT-2 Explorer

This is a demonstration of using the OpenAI GPT-2 language model to generate text. Enter some initial text and click "predict" to generate the most likely next words. Click on one of those words to choose it and continue. Click anywhere in the generated text to "roll back" to that point.

Built with love at the Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence (using Hugging Face's pytorch-pretrained-BERT library, so thanks to them).
